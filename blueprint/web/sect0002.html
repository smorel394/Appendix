Section A.2: compatible t-structures and the realization functor

We review the construction of the realization functor from [ 2 ] 3.1 and Appendix A of  [ 1 ] .

This section corresponds to the Lean file TStructure_no_proof.lean.

Compatible t-structures

We fix a filtered triangulated category \(\DF \) over a triangulated category \(\mathcal{D}\).

Definition .1

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.IsCompatible def-FilteredTriangulatedOver Suppose that we are given a t-structure \((\mathcal{D}^{\leq 0},\mathcal{D}^{\geq 0})\) on \(\mathcal{D}\) and a t-structure \((\DF ^{\leq 0},\DF ^{\geq 0})\) on \(\DF \). We say that these t-structures are compatible if \(i\colon \mathcal{D}\longrightarrow \DF \) is t-exact and if \(s^n(\DF ^{\leq a})\subset \DF ^{\leq b}\) for all \(a,b,n\in \mathbb {Z}\) such that \(a = b + n\).

Definition .2

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.compatible def-compatible Suppose that we are given a t-structure \((\mathcal{D}^{\leq 0},\mathcal{D}^{\geq 0})\) on \(\mathcal{D}\). Then there exists a t-structure \((\DF ^{\leq 0},\DF ^{\geq 0})\) on \(\DF \) such that

\[ \Ob \DF ^{\leq 0}=\{ X\in \Ob \DF \mid \forall i\in \mathbb {Z},\ \Gra ^i X[i]\in \Ob \mathcal{D}^{\leq 0}\} \]
\[ \Ob \DF ^{\geq 0}=\{ X\in \Ob \DF \mid \forall i\in \mathbb {Z},\ \Gra ^i X[i]\in \Ob \mathcal{D}^{\geq 0}\} . \]

Proposition .3

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.compatible_is_compatible prop-def-compatible Suppose that we are given a t-structure on \(\mathcal{D}\). Then it is compatible with the t-structure on \(\DF \) of Definition .2.

Proposition .4

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.compatible_uniq prop-def-compatible Suppose that we are given compatible t-structures \(t\) on \(\mathcal{D}\) and \(tF\) on \(\DF \). Then \(tF\) is equal to the t-structure of Definition .2.

Realization

We fix a filtered triangulated category \(\DF \) over a triangulated category \(\mathcal{D}\), as well as compatible t-structure \(t\) on \(\mathcal{D}\) and \(tF\) on \(\DF \), with hearts \(\mathcal{D}^\heartsuit \) and \(\DF ^\heartsuit \) and cohomology functors \(\H^ *_t\) and \(\H^ *_{tF}\). The realization functor is a triangulated functor \(\Db \mathcal{D}^\heartsuit \to \mathcal{D}\) extending the inclusion \(\mathcal{D}^\heartsuit \subset \mathcal{D}\).

To construct it, we first need to construct a functor \(H_F : \DF \to \Cb \mathcal{D}^\heartsuit \), which will induce an equivalence of categories when restricted to \(\DF ^\heartsuit \). In the paper, the \(n\)th degree of \(H_F(X)\) is defined as \(\H^ n_t(\Gra ^n(X))\). Given the new definition of \(\Gra ^n\), this is equal to \(\H^ n_t(\omega (\sigma _{[n,n]}(X)))\); that last formula makes it easier to construct the differentials.

Definition .1

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.FilteredToComplex_deg def-FilteredTriangulatedOver, def-Gr For \(n\in \mathbb {Z}\), we define a functor \(H_F^n:\DF \to \mathcal{D}^\heartsuit \) by \(\H_ F^n=\H^ n_t\circ \Gra ^n\).

Definition .2

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.FilteredToComplex_diff def-HF-deg, def-truncGELE_triangle For \(n\in \mathbb {Z}\), we define a natural transformation \(d^n : H_F^n\to H_F^{n+1}\) by setting \(d^n(X)\) to be the image by \(\omega \) of the degree \(1\) map in the triangle \(\sigma _{[n+1,n+1]}(X)\to \sigma _{[n,n+1]}(X)\to \sigma _{{n,n}}(X)\) of Definition .34.

Proposition .3

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.FilteredToComplex_condition def-HF-diff For every \(n\in \mathbb {Z}\), we have \(d^{n+1}\circ d^n = 0\).

Definition .4

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.FilteredToComplexObj prop-HF-condition This is the complex of functors \(\DF \to \mathcal{D}^\heartsuit \) whose degree \(n\) term is \(H_F^n\), and whose differentials are given by the \(d^n\).

Definition .5

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.FilteredToComplex def-HF” This is the functor \(H_F':\DF \to \mathrm{C}(\mathcal{D}^\heartsuit )\) corresponding to the complex of Definition .4.

We check that \(H_F'\) lands in the subcategory of bounded complexes.

Proposition .6

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.FilteredToComplex_isBounded def-HF’, prop-Gr_le, prop-Gr_ge For every \(X\) in \(\DF \), the complex \(HF'(X)\) is bounded.

Definition .7

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.FilteredToBoundedComplex prop-HF_bounded This is the functor \(H_F:\DF \to \Cb (\mathcal{D}^\heartsuit )\) given by \(HF'\).

Theorem .8

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.FilteredToBoundedComplex_equivalence def-HF, def-compatible The restriction to \(\DF ^\heartsuit \) of the functor \(\DF \) is an equivalence \(\DF ^\heartsuit \stackrel{\sim }{\longrightarrow }\Cb (\mathcal{D}^\heartsuit )\)

Proposition .9

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.Realization_aux thm-HF_equiv The composition of a quasi-inverse of the equivalence \(\DF ^\heartsuit \stackrel{\sim }{\longrightarrow }\Cb (\mathcal{D}^\heartsuit )\) of Theorem .8, of the inclusion \(\DF ^\heartsuit \subset \DF \) and of \(\omega :\DF \to \mathcal{D}\) sends quasi-isomorphisms to isomorphisms.

Definition .10

CategoryTheory.Triangulated.TStructure.Realization prop-real_aux The functor \(\real :\Db (\mathcal{D}^\heartsuit )\to \mathcal{D}^\heartsuit \) coming from the universal property of the localization \(\Cb (\mathcal{D}^\heartsuit )\to \Db (\mathcal{D}^\heartsuit )\) applied to the statement of Proposition .9.
